Privacy Policy — SiteLink Pro
Last updated: 8 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how SiteLink Pro ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use the SiteLink Pro field engineer mobile application.

SiteLink Pro is a business field-service application provided to authorised engineers by their employer. An account is required and is issued by your employer; the app is not available to the general public.

Who is responsible for your data
The app is operated on behalf of your employer (the organisation that issued your SiteLink Pro account). For any questions about this policy or your data, contact:

Email: david.burns@7-group.co.uk
Website: https://sitelink-software.co.uk
Information we collect
We only collect information needed to run the field-service workflow:

Account information — your name and email address, used to sign you in and identify your work.
Job and work data — jobs assigned to you, worksheet answers, materials requests, site variations, leave requests, agreements and signatures you create in the app.
Photos — site and job photos you choose to capture and upload against a job.
Location — only used at the moment you tap "Navigate" to open your maps app (Apple Maps, Google Maps or Waze) for directions to a job. We do not track your location in the background.
Technical/app activity — basic information needed for core functionality and to keep the app working reliably.
How we use your information
We use this information solely to:

provide the field-service features of the app (jobs, worksheets, materials, agreements, leave and messaging);
keep your office team in sync with your work;
maintain the security and reliability of the service.
We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell your data.

How your information is shared
Your information is shared with your employer's office team within the SiteLink Pro platform so they can manage and coordinate your work. It is processed by the service providers we use to operate the app, including:

cloud hosting and database providers (for storing your account and job data);
Google Cloud Storage (for storing uploaded files and photos);
our email provider (for sending account and notification emails).
We may also disclose information where required by law.

Data security
Data is encrypted in transit between the app and our servers. Access to your data is restricted to authorised users and service providers.

Data retention and deletion
We keep your information for as long as your account is active and as required for business and legal purposes. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, contact your employer's office team or email david.burns@7-group.co.uk.

Children
SiteLink Pro is a workplace application intended for adults (18+) and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above shows when it was last changed.

Contact
